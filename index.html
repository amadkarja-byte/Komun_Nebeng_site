Komun Nebeng Surabaya Pilot Project

Peer-to-Peer Transportation Coordination.

Komun Nebeng is a direct, community-driven tool designed to coordinate carpooling. We connect people looking for a ride with drivers who have empty seats, starting with early adopters in Surabaya.

Current Status: Beta / Pilot Phase

We are currently testing the matching process manually to ensure reliability. Because this is an early-stage pilot project, there are no automated algorithms, safety guarantees, or 100% matching promises. We focus entirely on facilitating direct coordination between members.

How It Works

Step 01

Register & Verify

Submit your profile and upload your official ID. Verification is required to promote transparency and trust within our community pool.

Step 02

Find a Driver

Look for active drivers heading your way, or list your route to let others know when and where you are commuting.

Step 03

Coordinate Directly

Once matched, we share contact handles. Sync schedules, confirm details, and coordinate via social media or messaging platforms.

Pilot Phase Disclaimers

  • No Safety Guarantees: We do not conduct professional background checks. Members must verify and coordinate at their own discretion.
  • Direct Peer-to-Peer: All fuel sharing or cost negotiations are handled directly between the hitchhiker and the rider/driver. Komun Nebeng handles no transactions.
  • Surabaya First: We are starting small, focusing on early adopters in Surabaya to refine the coordination model before considering expansion.
